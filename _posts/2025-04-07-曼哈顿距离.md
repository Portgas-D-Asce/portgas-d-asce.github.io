# 曼哈顿距离

平面上两个点之间的曼哈顿距离定义如下：
$$
\begin{aligned}
d_{ij} &= |x_i - x_j| + |y_i - y_j|\\
&= max(x_i - x_j, x_j - x_i) + max(y_i - y_j, y_j - y_i) \\
&= max(x_i - x_j + y_i - y_j, x_i - x_j + y_j - y_i, x_j - x_i + y_i - y_j, x_j - x_i + y_j - y_i)\\
&= max(|(x_i + y_i) - (x_j + y_j)|, |(x_i - y_i) - (x_j - y_j)|)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
d_1 = x + y \\
d_2 = x - y
$$





空间上两个点：
$$
d_1 = x + y + z \\
d_2 = x + y - z \\
d_3 = x - y + z \\
d_4 = x - y - z
$$
